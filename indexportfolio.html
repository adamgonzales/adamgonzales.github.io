Welcome to Adam Gonzales' Portfolio!

I am currently a senior at San Jose State University studying for my BA in Art and Design Studies, Concentration in Graphic Design. My interests within my field of study are branding, editorial/publication design, photography, and video editing. When I think of graphic design, I see it as art with a message and meaning. At the same time, it's about finding a creative solution to a problem or challenge. I believe that what we do in life should serve a purpose, be genuine, and serve well towards others. What I hope to achieve is to create good quality and meaningful work.
