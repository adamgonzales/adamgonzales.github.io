Meme

This is a Meme Mashup project for my ART 74 class. The idea was to put two images together that have absolutely no correlation, but somehow manage to be ironic and work well together. In this case, I took the helmet of the Doom Slayer from the 2016 video game "DOOM", and placed it on Mel Gibson's character from the film "Braveheart", combined into one meme.
